Features

Everything your clan needs

🏆
Automatic ranking
Automatically published each week after Clan War. Ranking, progression badges and points evolution.
📋
Staff Report
Automatic post-ranking alert on inactive players, negative members and well-deserved promotions.
👥
Enhanced member list
Displays all members with their badge, points, participation rate and clan join date.
📈
Progression graphs
Individual progression curves published in a dedicated channel after each Clan War.
🏖️
Absence management
Declare absence for a player. The bot automatically injects points and excludes the week from the ratio.
🔥
Participation tracking
Participation rate calculated week by week, absences automatically deducted.

How it works

Simple. Automatic. Efficient.

01
Setup
Provide your clan tag, Discord channels and staff role. That's it.
02
Clan War ends
RankBot automatically detects the end of the war and calculates results in real time.
03
Auto publish
Ranking, staff report and graphs published in the right channels. No action needed.
